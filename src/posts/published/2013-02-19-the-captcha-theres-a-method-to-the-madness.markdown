>If you spend any time at all on the Internet, you’ve likely struggled to decipher a CAPTCHA, the garbled text often found at the end of an online form.

>CAPTCHA is an acronym for Completely Automated Public Turing test to tell Computers and Humans Apart. In other words, it’s a tool designed to prevent spam by requiring a task that cannot be completed by a computer.
